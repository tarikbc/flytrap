Terms of Use

This is an educational demonstration. This Wi-Fi sign-in page is a simulated captive portal used to teach how such portals collect form input. It is not operated by, affiliated with, or endorsed by any brand shown.

No real accounts

Do not enter real usernames or passwords. Anything typed here is only for demonstrating how a portal captures form fields on the network you are authorized to test.

Authorized use only

This portal may only be run on networks and devices that the operator owns or has explicit written permission to test. Using it to capture other people’s credentials without consent is illegal in most places.

No warranty

The page is provided as-is for learning, with no warranty and no liability for misuse.

Privacy Notice

For education only. This simulated portal exists to show what a captive portal can see when you submit a form. Please do not enter real personal information.

What a portal like this can capture

Whatever is typed into the form fields and submitted is sent to the device running the demo and displayed to the operator, so they can see exactly how credential capture works. That is the entire point of the lesson.

Where it goes

Submitted fields are shown to the local operator and, in a real deployment, may be logged to their device. Nothing is sent to any third party or over the internet — this network has none.

Your takeaway

Treat every “sign in to Wi-Fi” page with suspicion. A real login never needs your social password to grant internet access.
